DIY Hydrofoil Surfboard

September 2020


While being confined at home we decided to take the opportunity to spend a lot of time sanding, playing with epoxy and trying our best to learn the joy of foiling on a surboard.

We built a hydrofoil out of fiberglass, foam and wood. We then retrofitted it on an old surfboard, with some optional underwater propellers and practiced foil boarding, foil pumping and wing foiling on the Charles River. Here is a collection of this adventure, the list of parts we purchased and our best effort at foil pumping.
