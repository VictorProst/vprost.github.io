Woodworking Projects


Cutting Board

May 2018


Purple heart inlay of a pineapple, the international symbol of welcoming in a bamboo board. This project involved the use of a CNC router with a V-bit to carve the bamboo board and the purple heart board. Of course, it also required a lot of sanding.
