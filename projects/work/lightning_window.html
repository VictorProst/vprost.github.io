Thunderstorm Effects Window

April 2016 - June 2016


Along with five others engineers I worked to develop a prototype window installation that could simulate a thunderstorm for an indoor mystery-manor themed adventure. The final product was capable of simulating flashes of lightning, the pour of rain, growling thunder, and a small surprise for unsuspecting viewers.

This work was performed for 5Wits through course 2.744 at MIT. The pictures and videos below highlight some of the outcomes.
