ABOUT ME

I am a highly motivated PhD candidate in Mechanical Engineering at MIT passionate about mechanical design innovations with global impact. I thrive in fast-paced work collaborative environments, am able to use fundamental engineering principles and quickly develop expertise to deliver on a variety of technical projects. I have 5 years of multi-disciplinary academic research experience and I am seeking design engineering opportunities in industry to apply deep analytical knowledge and practical expertise of mechanical design, prototyping, user-centered design, manufacturing, structural mechanics, precision machine design and optimization techniques.
