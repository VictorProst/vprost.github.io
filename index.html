I am currently a PhD student in Mechanical Engineering at MIT passionate about mechanical design innovations with global impact. I work in the Global Engineering and Research Lab (GEAR Lab) designing prosthetic feet for the developing world.

I received a M.Sc in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Mathematics from Ecole Polytechnique in June of 2015. I have a deep passion for design and engineering. I love taking gadgets apart to figure out how they work, look at them through the lens of fundamental engineering principles to see they can be improved, and create new impactful technologies.

This website contains projects that I have worked on as well as what I have learned from these projects and from mentoring others. See the about section for my contact information
